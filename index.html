My idea was formed on the road trip to Vermont during the fall break. My friend photobombed the beautiful maple leaves view by posturing like an artifact he bought in a thrift shop. (You probably familiar with the figure that I used!) When I looked back to my photo collection, I found many interesting places that I wanted to share with friends. I picked a bakery in Aachen, and the Hyde park in London and the Giant Causeway in Northern Ireland.
The website theme was inspired by Professor Ferro. She told me that this idea was like the teleportation machine in Star Trek so I adopted the idea by applying the computer interface in Star Trek.
While doing the altered reality project, I learned how to cast shadows through online tutorial and reviewed how to use color range to select object.
